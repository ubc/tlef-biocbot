Why students confuse Km and Vmax

Jane Liu · Apr 12, 2026

Students often mix up Km with binding affinity. Km is the substrate concentration at which the reaction rate is half of Vmax, not a direct affinity constant. I find it useful to start from the Michaelis-Menten plot...
