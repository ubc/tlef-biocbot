Super Chat Notes

A shared biochemistry knowledge base that the bot reads from. Any instructor can add notes — corrections, explanations, examples, things students keep asking about — in plain language. From then on, when anyone uses Super Chat, the bot draws on these notes alongside the uploaded course material. It's a department-wide repository, not tied to any single course. Other instructors can see your notes; students cannot.
